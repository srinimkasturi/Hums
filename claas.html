§ 01 · What CLAAS Is

A curated selection, not an invention

Honest positioning — this is the moat. CLAAS is not a methodology BL invented. It is a personally curated subset of existing organizational research — selected through 30 years of lived enterprise observation at IBM, TRW, Freddie Mac, and the World Bank Group. BL claims to have selected the subset that matters most right now, integrated it through one coherent diagnostic lens, and made it teachable. The 30-year credential paired with this honesty is the moat — not a universal theory claim.

CLAAS names what dimensions a T-shaped person needs to develop toward, in this era. It is historically situated. It will evolve. It is intentionally mortal — transferring at Year 8 of the Banyan Logic roadmap precisely because its dimensions will shift. HUMS™ is the permanent instrument underneath it.

The name evolved: the original ISACA December 2025 presentation was titled "Transformation @ Cyber @ Lean + Agile @ Scale" — CLASS without the A. Automation was added in 2026 to explicitly name the full spectrum: AI through robotics through all non-human intelligence.

§ 05 · BRD Six Story Types

CLAAS's most direct delivery expression

A Nurse Training guide only has user stories. C-SALF BRDs capture all six simultaneously. This is the CLAAS Cybersecurity dimension made operational in every phase.

User Story
As a [role], I need [capability] so that [outcome]. 3+ testable acceptance criteria.
Adversarial Attack
As a [threat actor], I would attempt [attack]. Every user story has a mirror. HUMS S06 Reflex · S09 See.
Resilience
System must continue [function] when [component] fails. RTO · RPO · degraded mode. HUMS S14 · S17.
Performance
As [role], I need [action] in [time] under [load]. SLA: response + throughput + error rate. HUMS S16 Circulate.
Capacity
System must handle [concurrent load]. Growth Y1–Y3. Ceiling + graceful degradation. HUMS S15 Digest.
Administrability
Admin must govern without engineering. Add/remove users. Config without deploy. HUMS S21 · S17.
